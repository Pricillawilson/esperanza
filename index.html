So you think you can dazzle?...Make your move! Group dance Competition

GRUPO MUSICA

“Music is … A higher revelation than all Wisdom & Philosophy”- Ludwig van Beethoven

ABOUT THE EVENT:


Battle of Bands


EVENT RULES :

  1. A band of minimum 4 members.
  2. Participants should bring their own instruments.
  3. A standard 5 piece drum kit will be provided.
  4. Time limit is of 10 mins (i.e 8 mins for performance and 2 mins for setting of instruments).
  5. No pre recorded music is allowed.
  6. Vulgarity of any sort will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Judges decision is final.
  8. Participants should produce their college id card at the time of registration.
