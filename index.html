Solo Instrumental Competition


EVENT RULES :

  6. Judges decision is final.

About The Event:


Come and Join our Solo Dance Competition

EVENT RULES :

So you think you can dazzle?...Make your move! Group dance Competition

EVENT RULES :


  1. It is a group event.

Battle of Bands
